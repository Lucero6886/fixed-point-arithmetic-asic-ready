# Project 5.5: Review And Comparison Of Polar Encoder N=8 And SC Decoder N=4

## 1. Objective

This project reviews and compares two important Polar-code hardware blocks:

1. Polar Encoder N=8
2. SC Decoder N=4

The goal is to understand the difference between encoding and decoding hardware in terms of architecture, verification complexity, synthesis complexity, and RTL-to-GDSII implementation results.

This review also prepares the next development step toward SC Decoder N=8.
